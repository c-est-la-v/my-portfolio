Projects

KV62: Discovering Tutankhamon

Created for the Virtual Reality course 2023-2024 at Politecnico of Torino, "KV62: Discovering Tutankhamon" is a Desktop-VR application (with potential developments for headset) that allows users to explore the tomb of Tuankhamun, engaging in interactive and active learning through various features. The app is available here. The Italian pitch is available here.

Chemical Chronicles

Created for the 2023-2024 Game Design and Gamification course at Politecnico of Torino, "Chemical Chronicles: The Shrinking Adventure" is a platform videogame whereby the user is a shrunk scientist who has to face numerous adventures to get back to its natural size. The desktop-playable prototype is available here. The pitch is available here.

Elemhands

Created for the 2023-2024 VR area within the student team "LevelUpLab" at Politecnico di Torino, "Elemhands" is a VR videogame, developed for Meta Quest 3, whereby simplified sign language is the key to solving puzzles. The pitch is available here. The prototype was showcased in the Indie Dungeon area at Milan Games Week 2024.

Super Sonic

Created for the 2023-2024 Future Storytelling course at Politecnico of Torino, "Super Sonic: A Supersonic Social Media Campaign" is a social media campaign for "More&Less", a Politecnico project funded by the European Union, aiming to a sustainable supersonic flight. The campaign was presented directly to the European Union funders. The report is available here.

Tale of Contour

Created for the 2023-2024 Computer Animation course at Politecnico di Torino, this project recreated the CGI Animated Spot using Blender. The final result is available here.

Breastfeeding VR

Created as part of my Master's thesis at Ontario Tech University (Canada) through ERASMUS+, this VR app for Meta Quest Pro teaches pregnant women proper breastfeeding practices through gamification. Three versions of the app were developed: controllers, hand-tracking, and proxy-based (low and high fidelity). A paper has been submitted to IEEE VR 2026.
